Introduction

This page describe how you can donate to Simone. Continuous donations will help run Simone.

Please be aware that donating does not give you any special power over the direction in which Simone's development go. It's just a token of your appreciation.

When you're donating, you're donating to Cezary Kluczyński, an original author of Simone.
